The latest research in mindfulness and education can be challenging to keep track of because there is so much of it and it cuts across many academic disciplines. In the "Learn" section you will find an updated collection of scholarly references and news on mindfulness to help educators learn about the latest research and how you might use it to inform your teaching. 

There are four types of content in this section: 

  • Research Studies
    Scholarly articles with summaries to help highlight the relevant ideas for educators
  • News Articles
    Timely articles from the media on topics related to mindfulness. 
  • Interviews
    Video interviews with teachers and scholars from around the globe with expertise in connecting mindfuless and education.
  • Tools
    Practical Resources to support the integration of mindfulness practices in to schools.

"Look Up" - Gary Turk

Look Up

 
00:00

In this spoken word film that has gone viral on the Internet, Gary Turk explores how technology may be distracting us from the reality of life around us. The film should raise questions about the purposes and uses of technology. In particular, a mindfulness approach encourages the questions, Is there a way to mindfully balance technology use and being present to those around us? How can we use technology to enhance our relationships with others while not missing out on the "real life" opportunities and experiences that present themselves each day?
